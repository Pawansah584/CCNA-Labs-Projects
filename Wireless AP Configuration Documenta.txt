Description: Sets up a wireless access point (AP) configuration.

Command Syntax: dot11 ssid MYNETWORK, vlan 10, encryption vlan 10 mode ciphers aes, interface dot11radio 0, ssid MYNETWORK

Uses: Provides secure wireless connectivity with VLAN segmentation and encryption.
